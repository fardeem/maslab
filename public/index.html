MASLAB

Science happens in the lab

So why is the classroom so focused on textbooks?

Untrained teachers and underfunded schools means students often identify science as being equal to ungodly amount of memorization. In the process they never get to see the real beauty of science: of discovering, of finding out, of experiencing the joy of problem solving.

As a result interest in science education is falling everyday

But,
why does it even matter?

The 4th Industrial Revolution and automation will wipe out 75 million jobs. Think about that.

75 Million

JOBS - GONE

Without a proper science education, the youth of tomorrow will be unemployable, anxious and unable to give back to society. School, which was supposed to train them for the real world will have failed.

So, what can we do?

Discovery

Deep Understanding
